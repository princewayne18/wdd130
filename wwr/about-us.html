History

Whitewater rafting company was founded in 1998 by Prince Dube who is said to be one of the greatest rafters in the ealy 2000s. Having had fun with the sport Prince Dube saw it fit to open his company 21 May 2000. the Company has since enjoyed a vast amount of market share ever since
